Manage estimation rule sets and associated rules

The estimation rule set is linked to a purpose of a particular metrology configuration. The estimation rules are mapped to the reading types matching the inputs (collected data) and outputs (computed data) of the metrology configuration. Depending on the reading types in the metrology configuration the relevant rules can be selected and their options restricted.
