Reset estimated readings after underlying data modification

Estimated readings are reset on a usage point output after modification of the underlying data in order to keep the output data up to date.

The estimated readings will only be reset on dependent channels after the modification of data on meters that are linked to the selected usage point or of the data on usage point channels that are used for the calculation of dependent channels.

1

Important

Changes in reading qualities on a device won’t lead to resetting estimated data on a usage point as long as the change in reading qualities (suspect flagging, confirmation, etc.) isn’t the result of a data change.
