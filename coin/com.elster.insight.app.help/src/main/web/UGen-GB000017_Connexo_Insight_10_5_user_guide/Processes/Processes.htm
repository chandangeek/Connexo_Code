Process management

Introduction

This chapter describes how business processes are managed in Connexo Insight. The business processes are created and designed in Connexo Flow and they can be started and monitored on objects in Connexo Insight. The related tasks are equally managed in Connexo Insight. The overall management of Connexo business processes happens in Connexo Admin. Refer to the Connexo Admin user guide for more detailed information about overall business process management in Connexo. For more information about creating and designing processes, consult the Connexo Flow user documentation.
