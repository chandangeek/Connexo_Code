Data validation

Introduction

Data validation ensures clean, high-quality data regardless of its size, data format, platform or technology. Valid data is required for appropriate analysis and use in business processes such as exchange with other market parties. It inspires confidence in data quality and measurement infrastructure because less corrections will be required afterwards due to incorrect data.

Data validation rules enable you to validate the quality of collected and aggregated data. Connexo Insight allows flexible definitions of what needs to be validated and how it must be validated.
