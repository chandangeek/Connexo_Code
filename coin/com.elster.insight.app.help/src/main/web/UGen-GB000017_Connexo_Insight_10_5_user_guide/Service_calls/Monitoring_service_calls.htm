Monitoring service calls

Navigate service calls

Service calls can have multiple levels of nested service calls, i.e. children.

You can see all service calls on the Service calls page.

Click one of the service calls to open its landing page. If it is a parent service call, the landing page provides an overview of its children and the details of the selected parent service call.

Click one of the child service calls to open its landing page. If it is not a parent service call, the landing page provides the selected service call’s details. From here, you can easily link back to the parent service call or to the object that is affected by the selected service call.

During your navigation, you can use the breadcrumb to see where you are in the service call structure and jump back to a higher level. There is a more detailed description of the mentioned service call pages under “View service calls”.
