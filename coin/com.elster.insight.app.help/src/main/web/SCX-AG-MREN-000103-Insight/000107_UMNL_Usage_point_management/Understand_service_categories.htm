Understand service categories

Service category is a CIM term defining the utility. On the market, service categories are also referred to as commodities. Depending on the service category, the usage point will have different attributes, meter roles and available customer attribute sets.

After installation (initAll) the next service categories are present in the system:

1.   Electricity.

2.   Gas.

3.   Water.

4.   Heat.

Service categories are installed by your Connexo delivery team. You canâ€™t edit them yourself but you can view them in the administration section.

Steps

1.   Go to Administration > Service categories.

The available service categories are listed in a table on top of the page.

2.   Select one of the service categories to see its details in the details pane under the table with service categories.

In the details you see the meter roles that are available to usage points in this service category.

Under the service category details pane the custom attribute sets for the selected service category are listed. For each custom attribute set its view and edit privileges are listed and you see whether it is time-sliced or not.

3.   Select one of the custom attribute sets to see its attributes in a details pane.
