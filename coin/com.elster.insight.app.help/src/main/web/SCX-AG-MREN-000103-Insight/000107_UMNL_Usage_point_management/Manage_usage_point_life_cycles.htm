Understand usage point life cycles

Usage point life cycles are meant to let the usage points evolve in line with the changing customer requirements. Every usage point in Connexo Insight  is associated with a usage point life cycle. Usage point life cycle management supports automatic or manual follow-up of usage points as they move through a set of states and transitions that are maintained by the operators. A number of states and transitions are available out-of-the-box and custom ones can be added by your Connexo delivery team. The usage point life cycle secures your business processes as it tells the operators what actions are possible in a specific state and it limits the possibility to select incorrect actions.
