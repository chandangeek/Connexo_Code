General layout

All pages in Connexo Insight  have a consistent layout. The general layout pages let you navigate to the different functionalities in the application.

Figure: Connexo layout.

 

A. Application switcher. Click to open the desired application like Connexo Admin, Connexo Facts, Connexo Flow, Connexo Insight , Connexo MultiSense.

B. Quick access bar with quick access to the Workspace, Usage points and Administration pages.

C. Collapse or expand the navigation menu. When collapsed only the icons remains visible.

D. Clicking the application name opens the Dashboard.

E. Search: Quick link to the search page. Same as when clicking the application name.

F. Online help and “About” information.

G. User centre. Here you find the functions that are specific for the logged on user. For instance, you can log out from Connexo.

H. Work area. The contents of this area are different on each page and depend on the selected functionality. In this area you are viewing and editing data in Connexo Insight .
