View the dashboard

Explore the dashboard

5

Steps

1.   Click Dashboard on the quick access bar or click the application title in the left corner next to the hamburger icon.

Information on the dashboard is grouped in different widgets allowing you to focus on what is important to you.

The following widgets are currently available on the dashboard page.

Widget

Description

My worklist

Compact list of items that have been assigned to you or to one of your workgroups. Select a value from the dropdown list on top to determine the kind of items that you want to see. The items are sorted by priority and then by due date. The most urgent items are on top. Only the five most urgent items are shown in this list and you can click each one of them to see its detail page. Below, there is an indication of the total number of items that are assigned to you and those that are assigned to one of your workgroups. You can click these links as well to see the corresponding pre-filtered list.

My flagged usage points

List of usage points that have been flagged because they temporarily need greater attention and easier access. See “Flag a usage point”.

My favourite usage point groups

List of usage point groups that have been marked as favourite because they temporarily need greater attention and easier access. See “Mark a usage point group as favourite”.

Refresh the dashboard

In the upper right corner of the dashboard page you can see when the shown information was last updated. Click Refresh to update all data on the dashboard page to see the latest status. The update time is adjusted.
