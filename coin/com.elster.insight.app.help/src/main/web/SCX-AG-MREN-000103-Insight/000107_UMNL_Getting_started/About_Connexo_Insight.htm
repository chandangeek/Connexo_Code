About Connexo Insight

What is Connexo Insight ?

Connexo Insight  is a web based application with a complex back end providing long-term data storage and management of the data delivered by data collection applications such as Connexo MultiSense.

It imports, validates, cleans and processes these data in line with customer specified rules to make them available for integrated analytics and exchange with many different utility systems such as billing applications, workforce management systems, prepayment systems, asset management systems and many others. All meter data management features are used and configured in Connexo Insight .

Connexo Insight  uses international industry standards to guarantee interoperability and a fast time-to-market.

Figure: Scalable data management.

 

Aggregation

Flexible rule-based data aggregation. Calculate, view and report usage and demand as measured by the meters associated with multiple usage points.

Validation, editing and estimation

Central validation management with extendible validation and estimation rules. Verify, edit, confirm or replace collected data to assure that all data is correct, reliable and free of discrepancies.

Data exchange

Easily leverage the data in Connexo Insight  in other applications such as demand response, utility analytics, prepayment and many others.

Performance

Read, store, process and exchange all data in four to six hour windows.

Data life-cycle management

Store your data with rigorous versioning and maintain data integrity.

What are the benefits of using Connexo Insight ?

Having properly managed meter data, utility leaders can make profound decisions, deploy exciting new technologies and get the best return out of their existing base of assets. The data enables utilities to predict system load and usage to avoid imbalances. Data analysis helps pinpointing over- or under-utilised infrastructure, improving system throughput, speeding service restoration after outages and much more. Connexo Insight  brings meter data management to the next level from a middleware layer to a true added value generator. In short, Connexo Insight  supports utilities in delivering a higher quality of service to customers and stakeholders at a lower total cost of ownership.

Flexibility of a tailored and scalable solution

Connexo Insight  is a set of end-to-end solutions tailored according to role, commodity and region. It is capable to adapt swiftly to your changing energy environment and answer your evolving global and regional business and regulatory needs.

SOX compliance

Long-term storage. Keep audit trail of all received, modified and deleted data. Connexo Insight is built with a focus on security.

Interoperability

Open application programming interface on a proven technology platform using international industry standards such as CIM IEC and MDUS to make sure that Connexo Insight  can easily be used with other systems from different vendors.

Cloud supported architecture

Reduces time to market and total cost of ownership in terms of hardware, maintenance, etc. while maximising scalability and return on investment.

Low total cost of ownership

  • Unified system.

  • Automated business processes.

  • Management by exception reducing the number of required operators.

  • Interface adapted to specific user roles leading to steep learning curves and lower training cost.
