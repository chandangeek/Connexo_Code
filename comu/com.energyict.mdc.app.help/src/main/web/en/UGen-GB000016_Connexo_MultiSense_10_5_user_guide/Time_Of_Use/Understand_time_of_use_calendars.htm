Understand time of use calendars

Time of use calendars are used to determine the rate depending on the time of day and the season the commodity is delivered.

  • On-peak: when demand is low and costs are low.

  • Off-peak: when demand is high and costs are high.

    Figure: Time of use calendars

     

Time of use calendars in Connexo MultiSense

You can manage time of use calendars on device types and devices in MultiSense. However to add new calendars you have to go to the Admin application. There you can configure a calendar importer to import new calendars. Once you configured the importer then you just have to put the files in the correct folder and the calendar will be imported into Connexo.

In MultiSense you then add them to your device types and then you can assign them to your devices of that device type. You can preview the time of use calendar in the Admin application or on the device that uses this calendar.
