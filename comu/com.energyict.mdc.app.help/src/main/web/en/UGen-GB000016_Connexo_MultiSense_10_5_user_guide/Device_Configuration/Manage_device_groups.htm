Manage device groups

View device groups

5

Steps

1.   Go to Devices > Device groups > click Device groups.

2.   Click the device group name to see the details page. This page shows all devices that are included in the selected device group.

3.   Change the displayed columns by clicking the Columns button above the results table. Check the columns you want to see and uncheck the columns you wish to hide.

Add a static device group

5

Steps

1.   Go to Devices > Device groups > click Device groups.

2.   Click Add device group.

3.   In step 1 of the wizard give a unique name for the device group and select static device group.

4.   Click Next.

5.   In step 2 complete the filter criteria in the menu on top of the page to filter out the devices you want to add to your group.

6.   Click Preview. The applied filters are shown on top of the page. You can remove a filter by clicking the remove icon 8 or click Clear all to remove all filters.

7.   Select All devices if you want to add all filtered device to the group or select Selected devices to select only some devices.

8.   Click Next.

9.   Confirm the selection by clicking Confirm. This page shows the amount of devices in the new device group.

10.   Click Finish.

Add a dynamic device group

5

Steps

1.   Go to Devices > Device groups > click Device groups.

2.   Click Add device group.

3.   In step 1 of the wizard give a unique name for the device group and select dynamic device group.

4.   Click Next.

5.   In step 2 complete the filter criteria in the menu on top of the page to filter out the devices you want to add to your group.

6.   Click Preview. The applied filters are shown on top of the page. You can remove a filter by clicking the remove icon 8 or click Clear all to remove all filters.

7.   Click Next.

8.   Confirm the device group by clicking Confirm. On this page you can see the amount of filter criteria used and the total amount of devices in the device group at this moment. New devices that comply with the filter criteria will automatically be added to this device group.

9.   Click Finish.

Edit a device group

5

Steps

1.   Go to Devices > Device group > Device groups.

2.   Click the actions icon  and select Edit.

3.   Complete the filter fields and click Preview.

a. For a static device group you can choose to select all devices returned by the filter or only select a couple.

b. For a dynamic device group all results will be added to the device group.

4.   Click Next.

5.   Confirm by clicking Confirm.

6.   Click Finish.

Remove a device group

5

Steps

1.   Go to Devices > Device group > Device groups.

2.   Click the actions icon  and select Remove.

3.   Click Remove.

3

Note

Device groups cannot be removed when they are in use for example in an export task.
