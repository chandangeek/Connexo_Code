Handle single alarms

Assign alarms

5

Steps

1.   Go to Workspace > Alarms > Alarms > click an alarm or click an alarm in the What’s going on widget on the device landing page or in the My worklist widget on the dashboard.

2.   Click the actions icon  next to the alarm you want to assign and select Assign to me to assign it to yourself or select Assign alarm to assign it to another user.

3.   If you select Assign alarm define the workgroup and the user.

4.   Click Assign.

Close alarm

5

Steps

1.   Go to Workspace > Alarms > Alarms > click an alarm or click an alarm in the What’s going on widget on the device landing page or in the My worklist widget on the dashboard.

2.   Click the actions icon  next to the alarm you want to close and select Close alarm.

3.   Select the status of the alarm when closing and provide a comment if needed.

4.   Click Close.

5.   This alarm will be removed from the table, but you can still see the alarm if you select Resolved and/or Won’t fix in the Status filter.

Snooze an alarm

You can snooze an alarm to remove it from all operational screens for a specific amount of time. This is handy if you decide that the alarm shouldn’t have your attention during that time. Note that you can also snooze multiple alarms simultaneously. See “Manage alarms in bulk”.

5

Steps

1.   Go to Workspace > Alarms > Alarms.

2.   Click the actions icon  next to the alarm, the actions button  in the details pane or click the alarm name and click the actions button on the details page.

3.   Click Snooze.

4.   Enter the date and time until when the selected item shouldn’t appear on the default overview table or on any operational screen. By default, the snoozing time is one day.

5.   Click Snooze.

The snoozed alarm is no longer visible on the alarm overview table with the default filter parameters. You can still retrieve the snoozed items by selecting Snoozed in the filter parameter Status.

You can change the snoozing parameters of a snoozed alarm by repeating the above procedure.

When the configured snoozing end time is reached, the snoozed status is removed and the selected issues becomes visible again on all operational screens.

1

Important

To check snoozing end time of snoozed alarms a recurring task is used. This task must be active in Connexo Admin > Application servers > Your application server > Message services. Contact your Connexo administrator for support or refer to the Connexo Admin user guide.

Add comment to alarm

5

Steps

1.   Go to Workspace > Alarms > Alarms > click an alarm or click an alarm in the What’s going on widget on the device landing page or in the My worklist widget on the dashboard.

2.   Click the actions icon  next to the alarm you want add a comment to and select Add comment or click the alarm name and select the Comments tab under Contextual information and click Add comment.

3.   Write the comment in the comment field.

4.   Click Add.

Edit or remove a comment on an alarm

You can edit and remove the comments that you added yourself to the alarm. Comments added by other users can only be edited by those users.

5

Steps

1.   Open the alarm for which you want to edit or remove the comment. See “View alarms”.

2.   Find the comment in the section Contextual information under the tab Comments at the bottom of the page.

3.   Click the pencil icon that you find at the end of the comment to edit the comment.

4.   Click Save to save your edits.

5.   Click the remove icon that you find at the end of the comment to remove the comment.

6.   Click Remove to confirm removal of the comment.

Set alarm priority

5

Steps

1.   Go to Workspace > Alarms > Alarms > click an alarm or click an alarm in the What’s going on widget on the device landing page or in the My worklist widget on the dashboard.

2.   Click the actions icon  next to the alarm of which you want to change the priority and select Set priority.

3.   Change the urgency and/or the priority. The priority is changed based on the new values of the urgency and priority.

4.   Click Save.

Start process on alarm

5

Steps

1.   Go to Workspace > Alarms > Alarms > click an alarm or click an alarm in the What’s going on widget on the device landing page or in the My worklist widget on the dashboard.

2.   Click the actions icon  next to the alarm you want to handle with a process and select Start process.

3.   Select a process from the list.

4.   Complete the process specific parameters.

5.   Click Start.

6.   All processes linked to this alarm are visible on the alarm details page in the Processes tab under Contextual information.
