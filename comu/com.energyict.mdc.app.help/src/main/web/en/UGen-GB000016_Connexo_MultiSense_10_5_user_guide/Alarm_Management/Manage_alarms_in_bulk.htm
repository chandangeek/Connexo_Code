Manage alarms in bulk

3

Note

Note that it is not possible to start processes on alarms in bulk.

Assign alarms in bulk

5

Steps

1.   Go to Workspace > Alarms > Alarms > click an alarm or click an alarm in the What’s going on widget on the device landing page or in the My worklist widget on the dashboard.

2.   Click the Bulk action button.

3.   In step 1 select which alarms to handle: all alarms or only specific alarms.

4.   Click Next.

5.   In step 2 select Assign alarms and click Next.

6.   In step 3 define the workgroup and user to assign the alarm(s) to. You can insert a comment if needed.

7.   Click Next.

8.   Click Confirm to confirm the action in step 4.

9.   Step 5 shows the status and result of the action. Click Finish.

Close alarms in bulk

5

Steps

1.   Go to Workspace > Alarms > Alarms > click an alarm or click an alarm in the What’s going on widget on the device landing page or in the My worklist widget on the dashboard.

2.   Click the Bulk action button.

3.   In step 1 select which alarms to handle: all alarms or only specific alarms.

4.   Click Next.

5.   In step 2 select Close alarms and click Next.

6.   In step 3 define the reason for closing the alarm(s). You can insert a comment if needed.

7.   Click Next.

8.   Click Confirm to confirm the action in step 4.

9.   Step 5 shows the status and result of the action. Click Finish.

Set priority to multiple alarms simultaneously

You can set the priority to multiple alarms at the same time to save time.

5

Steps

1.   Go to Workspace > Alarms > Alarms > click an alarm or click an alarm in the What’s going on widget on the device landing page or in the My worklist widget on the dashboard.

2.   Click the Bulk action button.

3.   In step 1 select which alarms to handle: all alarms or only specific alarms.

4.   Click Next.

5.   In step 2 select Set priority and click Next.

6.   In step 3 enter the Urgency and the Impact for the selected alarms. This will calculate the priority in the field Priority.

7.   Click Next.

8.   In step 4 verify the prepared action that and click Confirm.

9.   Finally, step 5 shows the result of the action.

10.   Click Finish to return to the alarms overview.
