Favourite device(s) (groups)

View flagged devices

5

Steps

1.   Go to the Dashboard.

2.   All flagged devices are shown in the pane with the name My flagged devices. The flagged devices are shown in chronological order showing the last flagged devices first.

When you hover over the flagged device some specific features are shown together with the comment you added when flagging the device.

3.   Click the grey star to remove the device from your favourite devices.
