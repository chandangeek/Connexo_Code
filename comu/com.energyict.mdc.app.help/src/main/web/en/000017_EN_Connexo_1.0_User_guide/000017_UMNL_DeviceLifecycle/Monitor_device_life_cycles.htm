Monitor device life cycles on a device type

View device life cycle on a device type

5

Steps

1.   Go to Administration > Device management > Device types > select a device type.

You can see the device life cycle used by the device in the details pane below. When you click the name of the device life cycle, you will go to the overview page of this device life cycle.

You are also able to see the device life of this device when you click the name of the device and go to the its overview page.

View which device types use the device life cycle

5

Steps

1.   Go to Administration > Device life cycle management > Device life cycles > select a device life cycle.

You can see which device types use the device life cycle in the details pane on the bottom of the page or you can see this list on the overview page of the device life cycle when you click its name. Click the name of the device type to go to its overview page.
