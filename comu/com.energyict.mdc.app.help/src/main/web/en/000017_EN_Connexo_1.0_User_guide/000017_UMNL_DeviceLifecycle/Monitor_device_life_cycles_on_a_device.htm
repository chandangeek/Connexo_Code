Monitor device life cycles on a device

View device life cycle history on a device

5

Steps

1.   Select a device using Search, see “Advanced search”.

2.   Click History in the menu on the left or click View history next to the device status on the device landing page.

The Device life cycle tab shows when and which user changed the device state and device life cycle.

Device options and the device life cycle state

Depending on the state of the device life cycle the device is currently residing in, different actions may not be possible or some information will not be displayed. These options mainly concern in stock devices and decommissioned devices. The table below shows an overview of the possibilities on these devices:

o   Open issues not shown

  • Communication tasks not shown

  • Communication topology not shown.
