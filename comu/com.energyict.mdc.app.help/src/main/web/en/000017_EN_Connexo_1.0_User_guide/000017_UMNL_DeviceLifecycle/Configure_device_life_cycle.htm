Configure device life cycle

Manage device life cycles

Add device life cycle

5

Steps

1.   Go to Administration > Device life cycle management > Device life cycles.

2.   Click Add device life cycle and define a name for the life cycle.

3.   Click Add.

The newly created device life cycle is based on the default device life cycle.

Clone device life cycle

5

Steps

1.   Go to Administration > Device life cycle management > Device life cycles.

2.   Click the actions icon  next to the device life cycle you want to clone and select Clone.

3.   Name the new device life cycle and click Clone.

This newly created device life cycle has all the same states and transitions as the device life cycle you cloned.

Edit device life cycle

5

Steps

1.   Go to Administration > Device life cycle management > Device life cycles.

2.   Click the actions icon  and select Edit.

3.   Change the name.

4.   Click Save.

Remove device life cycle

5

Steps

1.   Go to Administration > Device life cycle management > Device life cycles.

2.   Click the actions icon  and select Remove.

3.   Click Remove.

3

Note

It is not possible to remove device life cycles that are still in use by one or more device types.

Change the device life cycle of a device type

1

Important

When changing a device life cycle the new device life cycle must contain all states that are used by devices configured with this device type.

5

Steps

1.   Go to Administration > Device management > Device types.

2.   Click the actions icon  and select Change device life cycle.

3.   In step one of the wizard select the new device life cycle.

4.   Click Next.

5.   Step two of the wizard shows the status of the process. Click Finish when the action was successful or click Retry when changing the device life cycle failed.
