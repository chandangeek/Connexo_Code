Process management

This chapter describes how business processes are managed inside the MultiSense  application. The business processes themselves are created and designed in Connexo Flow . However, all processes that are available in MultiSense  are also visible in MultiSense . Processes can be started and monitored on objects and the corresponding tasks can be managed in Connexo MultiSense. The complete overview and management of processes is done in Connexo Admin.

For more information on the creation and design of processes, consult the Connexo   Flow  user documentation (http://docs.jboss.org/jbpm/v6.4/userguide/).
