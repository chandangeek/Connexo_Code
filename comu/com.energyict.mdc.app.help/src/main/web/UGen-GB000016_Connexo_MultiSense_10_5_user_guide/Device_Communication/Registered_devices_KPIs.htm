Registered devices KPIs

Using the registered devices KPIs you can see how many slaves in a device group are connected to their master device at a particular point in time and compare this to a predefined target. You can configure the KPIs calculation frequency. The output of this KPI can be seen in Workspace > Data communication > Registered devices.

View the available registered devices KPIs

You can see a complete overview of all registered devices KPIs that are currently available in Connexo MultiSense.

5

Steps

1.   Go to Administration > KPIs > Registered devices KPIs.

The table shows all configured registered devices KPIs by the device group they apply to. You also see how often the data quality KPI is calculated and when it was last calculated. You can also see the configured benchmark target to compare the actual situation to.

Add a registered device KPI

You can add a registered device KPI to see how many slaves in a device group are connected to their master device at a particular point in time.

5

Steps

1.   Go to Administration > KPIs > Registered devices KPIs.

2.   Click Add registered devices KPI.

3.   Complete the following fields:

Parameter

Description

Device group

Select the device group that contains the devices for which the registered devices KPI must be calculated. You can only create one registered devices KPI per device group. Device groups that are already associated with a registered devices KPI aren’t listed in the drop-down list.

Calculation frequency

Select how often the KPI must be recalculated.

Target

Enter a percentage that will appear on the KPI’s output as a benchmark to compare the actual situation to. The percentage represents the objective of registered devices.

4.   Click Add.

The new registered devices KPI is now listed in the overview table.

Change the target of a registered devices KPI

You can change the configuration of a registered devices KPI to better match your monitoring needs.

1.   Go to Administration > KPIs > Data quality KPIs.

2.   Select Edit from the actions icon in the line of the KPI that you want to edit.

Only the value in the field Target can be edited.

3.   Enter the correct registered devices objective as a percentage in the field Target.

4.   Click Save.

Remove a registered devices KPI

You can remove a registered devices KPI if it is no longer relevant.

5

Steps

1.   Go to Administration > KPIs > Data quality KPIs.

2.   Select Remove from the actions icon in the line of the KPI that you would like to remove.

3.   Click Remove to confirm.

The registered devices KPI is no longer available from the overview table.

See the output of the registered devices KPI

The output of the registered devices KPI calculates and stores the amount of slaves in a device group that are connected to their master device at a given point in time.

The information is presented in a graph that shows the evolution of registered devices over time.

5

Steps

1.   Go to Workspace > Data communication > Registered devices.

2.   Use the filter bar on top to see the registered devices KPI output that is relevant to you. The available device groups in this filter are those that have been defined in the configuration of the registered devices KPI.

The X-axis of the shown graph shows the timestamps that were defined in the KPI’s configuration. The Y-axis shows the number of registered devices for each of the timestamps.

There are two benchmark lines to compare the actual values to:

  • The green line shows the total amount of devices in the device group at the moment of the interval. This value may vary in a dynamic group or when adding devices to a static group. Hover over this line to see date, time and corresponding value.

  • The blue line shows the configured target for registered devices. Hover over this line to see date, time and corresponding value.

Hover over a block on the graph to see date, time and the total number of registered devices in this timeslot.

Use the handles on the timescale under the graph to broaden or narrow the viewed timespan.

The results in the overview table will be refreshed in line with the calculation frequency that is specified in the data quality KPI. See “Add a registered device KPI”.

See the output of registered devices KPIs on a gateway

You can view the output of a registered devices KPI on a gateway to see how many registered devices have been linked to the gateway at a given point in time.

5

Steps

1.   Open a gateway device.

2.   Click Registered devices in the group Communication in the left-hand column.

3.   Use the filter bar on top to see the registered devices KPI output that is relevant to you.  The available device groups in this filter are those that have been defined in the configuration of the registered devices KPI.

The X-axis of the shown graph shows the timestamps that were defined in the KPI’s configuration. The Y-axis shows the number of registered devices for each of the timestamps.

  • The green line shows the total amount of devices in the device group at the moment of the interval. This value may vary in a dynamic group or when adding devices to a static group. Hover over this line to see date, time and corresponding value.

  • The blue line shows the configured target for registered devices. Hover over this line to see date, time and corresponding value.

Hover over a block on the graph to see date, time and the total number of registered devices in this timeslot.

Use the handles on the timescale under the graph to broaden or narrow the viewed timespan.
