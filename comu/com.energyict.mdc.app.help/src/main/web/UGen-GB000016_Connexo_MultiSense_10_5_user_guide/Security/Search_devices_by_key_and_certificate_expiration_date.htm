Search devices by key and certificate expiration date

You can easily find devices with keys or certificates that are expired or that are about to expire.

5

Steps

1.   Click Search in the top corner of any Connexo page to open the search page.

2.   Click the button Add criteria and select Keys and certificates validity from the group Security.

On the search bar with active criteria on top of the search page, the Keys and certificates validity button is added.

3.   Click the Keys and certificates validity button on the search bar and select the needed value.

  • Expired.

  • Expires within a week.

  • Expires within a month.

  • Expires within 3 months.

  • Obsolete.
