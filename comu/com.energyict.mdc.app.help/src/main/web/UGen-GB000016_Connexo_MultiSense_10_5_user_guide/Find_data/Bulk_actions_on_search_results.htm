Bulk actions on search results

To save time you can do actions on multiple devices simultaneously directly from your search results. The actions function the same way as they do for single devices.

5

Steps

1.   Click the search buttonin the right upper corner of any page in Connexo MultiSense.

2.   Enter your search criteria and click Search.

3.   Click the button Bulk action on top of the table that shows the search results.

4.   In Select devices specify the devices that will be impacted by your action. Then, click Next.

 

1

Important

In step two of the bulk action wizard the selected actions can be applied to all items in the search results or to a number of hand-picked items from that list. So, All devices does not mean all devices in the system but all devices taking into account the search criteria you specified before.

5.   In Select action, indicate the action that will be done on the selected devices. Then, click Next.

6.   in Action details, you can enter the specific parameters that are needed to do the action. Then, click Next

7.   In Confirmation, you get a summary of what will be done. Click Confirm to start the bulk action.

8.   In Status you can follow up on the status of the started bulk action.

9.   Click Finish to end the bulk action.
