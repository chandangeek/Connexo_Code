Manage device groups

View device groups

5

Steps

1.   Go to Devices > Device groups > click Device groups.

2.   Click the device group name to see the details page. Details are also shown on the bottom of the page.

Add a static device group

5

Steps

1.   Go to Devices > Device groups > click Device groups.

2.   Click Add device group.

3.   In step 1 of the wizard give a unique name for the device group and select static device group.

4.   Click Next.

5.   In step 2 complete the filter criteria in the menu on the left to filter out the devices you want to add to your group.

6.   Click Apply. The applied filters are shown on top of the page. You can remove a filter by clicking the remove icon8 or click Clear all to remove all filters.

7.   Select All devices if you want to add all filtered device to the group or select Selected devices to select only some devices.

8.   Click Add.

Add a dynamic device group

5

Steps

1.   Go to Devices > Device groups > click Device groups.

2.   Click Add device group.

3.   In step 1 of the wizard give a unique name for the device group and select dynamic device group.

4.   Click Next.

5.   In step 2 complete the filter criteria in the menu on the left to filter out the devices you want to add to your group.

6.   Click Apply. The applied filters are shown on top of the page. You can remove a filter by clicking the remove icon8 or click Clear all to remove all filters.

7.   Click Add. The device group has been added to the system. New devices that comply with the filter criteria will automatically be added to this device group.
