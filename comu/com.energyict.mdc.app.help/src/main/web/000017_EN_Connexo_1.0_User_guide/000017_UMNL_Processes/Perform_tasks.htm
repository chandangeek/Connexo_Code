Perform tasks

Perform bulk actions on tasks

5

Steps

1.   Go to Workspace > Task management > Tasks or select one of the other views.

2.   Filter tasks if needed.

3.   Click Bulk actions.

4.   In step 1 select the tasks, select all tasks from the with or without filtering or make a custom selection.

5.   Click Next.

6.   Select the action in step 2.

7.   Click Next.

8.   In step 3 specify the parameters of the action selected in step 2.

9.   Click Next.

10.   Confirm the action in step 4 by clicking Confirm.

11.   Step 5 shows the result of the action, click Finish.
