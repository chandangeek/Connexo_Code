Process management

Introduction

This chapter describes the management of business processes in Connexo. From the central process overview you can activate, deactivate and edit processes. For starting and viewing the processes and their corresponding tasks, we refer to the Connexo MultiSense user guide.

For more information on the creation and design of processes, consult the Connexo Flow user documentation (http://docs.jboss.org/jbpm/v6.4/userguide/).
