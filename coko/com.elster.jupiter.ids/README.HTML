com.elster.jupiter.ids

Bundle provides timeseries support for both regular and irregular timeseries. (A regular timeseries has entries at regular intervals, e.g every minute, every half hour , every day , every month ..., while an irregular timeseries does not have a fixed interval) It registers a dataModel with the IDS acronym (interval data store) Note that the although the bundle's master data is handled by the ORM framework, persistence of the timeseries data is handled directly by this bundle.
